General Questions

What is EGO Express?

+

EGO Express is a premier freight forwarding and logistics company specializing in door-to-door cargo transport between the UAE, Kuwait, and expanding to other regions. We handle everything from small packages to large commercial shipments with complete care and professionalism.

What areas do you serve?

+

Currently, we offer services between:

  • All Emirates in the UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, RAK, Fujairah, UAQ)
  • Kuwait
  • Saudi Arabia (Coming Soon)

We're continuously expanding our network to serve more destinations.

How do I book a shipment?

+

Booking is simple! Just visit our booking page, fill in your shipment details including origin, destination, cargo information, and upload photos. Our team will review your request and send you a customized quote within 1 hour.

How quickly can you pick up my cargo?

+

Once you confirm your booking, we can typically arrange pickup within 24-48 hours. For urgent shipments, same-day pickup may be available in select areas - please contact us to check availability.

Do you provide packaging materials?

+

Yes! We offer professional packaging services including:

  • Standard packaging (included free)
  • Reinforced plastic wrap for extra protection
  • Wooden crates for fragile or valuable items
  • Bubble wrap and cushioning materials

Our team will recommend the best packaging solution for your specific cargo.

Is my cargo insured?

+

Absolutely! All shipments are fully insured from pickup to delivery. We offer comprehensive coverage to protect your cargo against loss, damage, or theft during transit. Additional insurance options are available for high-value items.

Can I track my shipment?

+

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number once your shipment is picked up. You can track your cargo in real-time and receive updates via SMS and email at every major milestone of the journey.

What if I need to change my delivery address?

+

Please contact us as soon as possible. Address changes can usually be accommodated if the shipment hasn't reached the final destination. Additional fees may apply depending on the new location and current shipment status.

Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

+

Delivery times vary by route:

  • UAE to Kuwait: 5-7 business days
  • Within UAE: 2-3 business days
  • Express service: Available for urgent shipments

Actual delivery time depends on customs clearance and destination accessibility.

What items cannot be shipped?

+

We cannot ship prohibited items including:

  • Illegal substances or contraband
  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Hazardous materials (without proper documentation)
  • Perishable food items (case by case)
  • Live animals

If you're unsure about an item, please contact us before booking.

Do you handle fragile items?

+

Yes! We specialize in handling fragile and valuable items. Simply mark your shipment as fragile when booking, and we'll ensure special care including reinforced packaging, careful loading, and priority handling throughout transit.

Is there a weight or size limit?

+

We can handle shipments of virtually any size! From small packages to large commercial cargo, furniture, and vehicles. For oversized or extremely heavy items, please provide detailed dimensions when booking so we can arrange appropriate equipment.

Do you offer door-to-door service?

+

Yes! Complete door-to-door service is our specialty. We pick up from your location and deliver directly to the recipient's address. No need to visit depots or warehouses - we handle everything.

Can I ship to a P.O. Box?

+

For large cargo shipments, we require a physical address for delivery. However, for smaller packages and documents, P.O. Box delivery may be available in certain areas. Please specify when booking.

Pricing Questions

How is shipping cost calculated?

+

Shipping costs are based on several factors:

  • Weight and volume of cargo
  • Origin and destination locations
  • Type of goods being shipped
  • Packaging requirements
  • Speed of delivery (standard or express)

We provide transparent, detailed quotes with no hidden fees.

Are there any hidden fees?

+

Never! We believe in complete transparency. Your quote includes all costs: pickup, transport, packaging, insurance, and delivery. The only additional charges would be for optional services you choose or unexpected customs duties.

Do I pay before or after shipping?

+

Payment terms are flexible. Typically, we require 50% deposit upon booking confirmation and the remaining 50% before final delivery. For regular corporate clients, we offer credit terms. Cash on delivery options may be available.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Bank transfers
  • Credit/Debit cards
  • Cash payments
  • Online payment platforms

Do you offer discounts for bulk shipments?

+

Yes! We offer competitive rates for regular customers and bulk shipments. If you ship frequently or have large volume requirements, contact us to discuss customized pricing packages tailored to your needs.

Customs & Documentation

Do you handle customs clearance?

+

Absolutely! Our experienced customs brokerage team handles all customs documentation and clearance procedures. We ensure your shipment complies with all regulations and moves through customs smoothly and quickly.

What documents do I need to provide?

+

Common documents required include:

  • Commercial Invoice (for commercial goods)
  • Packing List
  • Certificate of Origin (if applicable)
  • Copy of passport/Emirates ID

Our team will guide you on specific requirements based on your shipment type.

Who pays customs duties?

+

Customs duties are typically paid by the recipient unless otherwise arranged. We'll inform you of estimated duties during the quote process. You can choose to pay duties upfront or have the recipient pay upon delivery.

How long does customs clearance take?

+

With proper documentation, customs clearance typically takes 1-3 business days. However, this can vary based on the type of goods, destination country regulations, and customs inspection requirements. We monitor the process closely and keep you updated.
