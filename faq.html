General Questions

What is EGO Express?

+

EGO Express is a premier freight forwarding and logistics company specializing in door-to-door cargo transport between the UAE, Kuwait, and expanding to other regions.

Can I track my shipment?

+

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number once your shipment is picked up. You can track your cargo in real-time through our system.

What areas do you serve?

+

We currently serve UAE and Kuwait with door-to-door services, and we're expanding to additional Gulf countries and international destinations.

What types of cargo do you handle?

+

We handle various types of cargo including personal effects, commercial goods, electronics, furniture, and more. Some items may have restrictions or require special handling.

How can I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or WhatsApp. Visit our 'Contact Us' page for all contact details and business hours.

Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

+

Delivery times vary by destination. Typically, UAE to Kuwait takes 3-5 business days. We'll provide an estimated delivery date when you book your shipment.

Do you offer pickup services?

+

Yes! We offer door-to-door service including pickup from your location and delivery to the destination address. Schedule a pickup when booking your shipment.

What packaging is required?

+

Items should be properly packed in sturdy boxes with adequate cushioning. We also offer professional packing services if needed. Fragile items require special packaging.

What are the weight and size limits?

+

We can handle shipments of various sizes. For oversized or overweight cargo, please contact us for a custom quote and special handling arrangements.

Can I ship fragile items?

+

Yes, we handle fragile items with special care. Make sure to label them clearly as 'Fragile' and use appropriate packaging. Additional insurance is recommended for valuable fragile items.

Pricing Questions

How is shipping cost calculated?

+

Shipping costs are based on weight, dimensions, destination, and service type. You can get an instant quote through our booking page by entering your shipment details.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and digital payment methods. Payment is required before shipment pickup or upon booking confirmation.

Are there any hidden fees?

+

No, we provide transparent pricing. All costs including customs duties (if applicable) will be clearly communicated before you confirm your booking.

Do you offer discounts for bulk shipments?

+

Yes, we offer special rates for regular customers and bulk shipments. Contact our sales team to discuss volume discounts and corporate packages.

Is insurance included in the price?

+

Basic coverage is included, but we recommend purchasing additional insurance for high-value items. Insurance options will be presented during the booking process.

Customs Questions

Do you handle customs clearance?

+

Yes, we handle all customs documentation and clearance procedures on your behalf. Our team ensures smooth processing and compliance with all regulations.

What documents are required for customs?

+

Typically, you'll need a commercial invoice, packing list, and identification documents. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your shipment.

Are there items prohibited from shipping?

+

Yes, certain items like hazardous materials, weapons, illegal substances, and perishables cannot be shipped. Contact us for a complete list of prohibited items.

Who pays customs duties and taxes?

+

Customs duties and taxes are typically paid by the recipient. We'll inform you of any applicable charges before delivery. Some shipments may be exempt based on value or type.

What happens if my shipment is held at customs?

+

If your shipment is held, we'll contact you immediately with the reason and required action. Our customs team will assist you in resolving any issues quickly.
